## Quick Start

### Requirements

- Windows 10 / 11 (x64)
- Counter-Strike 2

### Launch

1. Download **Mozority Loader** from the [Download](https://mozority.pro/download/loader.exe) page.
2. Run the loader.
3. Enter your license key.
4. Launch **Counter-Strike 2** and wait until you load into the lobby or server.
5. In the loader, click **Inject**.
6. Press **INSERT** in the game to open the menu.

> [!TIP]
> You can obtain a key on the [mozority.pro](https://mozority.pro) website or from the administration.
